About

PubTest sets out to canvas the thoughts of the everydayAustralian and be transparent with the resuts.

We believe:

  • While there is nuance and complexity to every discussion, an opinion is inherently for or against (e.g. as seen in referendums).
  • Big data (i.e. no personally-identifiying data) affords anonymity, and in return, allow for more honest responses.
  • Give-and-take: The more data you provide, the more you will receive.
  • Paired with the question, a summary and a news article are provided for context. This caters for various cognitive types i.e. quick vs thoughtful thinkers.
